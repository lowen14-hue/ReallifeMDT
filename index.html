Logline

A boxer living his dream life glitches into a post-nuclear dystopian world where everyone wears helmets. He must do all he can to wake the Walkers.

Synopsis

Chaz, a world-renowned boxer, begins switching between his dream life and a dystopia where humans march in formation wearing reflective helmets that show dream worlds.

He awakens three others and together they uncover the truth behind the helmet system, survive “The Gauntlet,” and attempt to rebel.

Their revolt leads to a final confrontation with the President, who claims she is saving humanity after nuclear war. Overwhelmed, Chaz kills her — and his ally.

He reaches toward his helmet… BUZZER. An older Chaz awakens in a mall Helmet Shop. What was real?
